My Two Pugs

My two pugs are called Gary and Agnes.

Here is a list of Gary's favorite things:

  • Sleeping
  • Eating
  • Laying on your lap
  • Chasing his sister
  • Barking at birds and airplanes

Here is a list of Agnes's favorite things:

  • Snoring
  • Chewing on bones
  • Eating (sometimes her brother's food)
  • Chasing her brother
  • Playing tug with her many toys
