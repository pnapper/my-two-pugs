My Two Pugs

My two pugs are called Gary and Agnes.

Here is a list of Gary's favorite things:

  • Sleeping
  • Eating
  • Laying on your lap
  • Chasing his sister
  • Barking at birds and airplanes

Here is a list of Agnes's favorite things:

  • Snoring
  • Chewing on bones
  • Eating (sometimes her brother's food)
  • Chasing her brother
  • Playing tug with her many toys

Here is a list of things they both can agree on:

  1. Mom's lap is the best
  2. Mom's cooking is the best
  3. Daddy plays the best
  4. Beef ribs from Jack's are amazing!
  5. We have the best family ever!
